Tableau Project 3: Foreign Direct Investment Data Analysis

Softwares

Tableau Desktop, MS Excel

Dataset

Kaggle Dataset-Financial Year wise FDI

Date

April 9, 2021

In this Case Study, we will explore the FDI dataset and analyse it from the perspective of the business. We shall look at various sectors to determine which sector will prove most beneficial to collaborate with, for the company. This dataset contains sector and financial year wise data of FDI in India.

Acknowledgements

Ministry of Commerce and Industry has published Financial Year wise FDI Equity Inflows from 2000-01 to 2016-17 dataset in Open Government Data Platform India under Govt. Open Data License - India. Dataset
